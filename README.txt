The is simulation of Stochastic Method of AoV for the Philippine Mindanao Grid.

Programmed by: Engr. John Michael P. Corbeta, REE

The stochastic method and fault type probability data is from:

	J. M. Napao, C. A. Yap, B. Agustin, and J. R. Orillaza, “Stochastic
	analysis on the effect of fault impedance and fault type to the area
	of vulnerability,” in 2015 IEEE PES Asia-Pacific Power and Energy
	Engineering Conference (APPEEC), 2015, pp. 1–5.

How to use:

	Run main.m in MATLAB.
